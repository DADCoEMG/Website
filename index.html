Who are we?

DADCo is a biofeedback startup working in conjunction with Rochester Institute of Technology's Innovation Center. We seek to bring Electromyography (EMG) into the mainstream consumer marketplace by creating a diverse line of electrode-integrated smart devices and accessories. We believe that our products will revolutionize the wearable technology industry by creating a new way for users to interact with technology and potentially help thousands of people.

DADCo forsee's a multitude of useful applications for consumer EMG products. Some of these applications include:

  • Developing a new form of motion capture that doesn't require cameras
  • Interacting with objects in augmented and virtual reality
  • Controlling media devices such as phones without directly touching a screen
  • Monitoring fitness activites and giving fitness feedback
  • Providing expensive medical diagnostic procedures from the comfort of the consumers' home
  • Monitoring servicemen injuries in real time
  • Working closely with prosthetic companies such as OpenBionics to create affordable prosthetics
